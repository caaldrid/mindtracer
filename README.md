# Mindtrace
> A simple web app that follows the [P.A.R.A Method](https://fortelabs.com/blog/para/) to store all the information you need for your day-to-day life.

## Features
- Manage Projects with individual task and related resource linking while viewing your completion rate through a Github like activity grid.
- Keep track of all the Areas that are important in your life.
    - Currently, only has journal entries with mood tagging.
- Never loose an important article, document, or recipe through your currated Resource list.
- Archive what is no longer relevant to your current life without having to restart your research if you need it again.
